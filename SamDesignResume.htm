experience

I started my design career at Chillibush in 2008 as an intern, moving on to Graphicor later that year, moving up into a junior designer role. I saw my Mid-weight Designer years out at Cre8tive Worx from 2010 to 2012 and finally joined the Artifact family later that year. Having spent close to 7 years perfecting my craft as a Senior Designer at Artifact and being promoted into the Creative Lead role has given me the opportunity to create crafted collateral across a range of clients, both international like Kohler, Nissan, Starbucks and Galito’s as well as local brands such as JTI, Telkom and the Fish & Chip Co. I know what it means to work hard and perfect solutions for any brand on an individual basis.
